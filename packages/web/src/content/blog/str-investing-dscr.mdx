## STR Financing Is Different

Short-term rental properties (Airbnb, VRBO, vacation rentals) can generate significantly more income than traditional long-term rentals. But financing them requires a different approach.

Traditional DSCR loans use **long-term lease rates** to calculate the coverage ratio. STR-specific DSCR programs use **projected short-term rental revenue** — which is often 2-3x higher than long-term rent.

## How STR-DSCR Is Calculated

**STR-DSCR = Net Monthly STR Income / Monthly PITI**

Net monthly STR income accounts for:
- Gross monthly revenue (from AirDNA, PriceLabs, or T12 actuals)
- Operating expenses (cleaning, management, supplies, platform fees)
- Vacancy rate (typically 20-30% for STR)

## What Lenders Want to See

### Revenue Documentation
- **T12 Actuals** — 12 months of actual rental income (strongest)
- **AirDNA Projections** — Third-party market data (acceptable for purchases)
- **PriceLabs / Mashvisor** — Additional revenue documentation sources

### Property Requirements
- STR-eligible location (check local regulations)
- Furnished or to-be-furnished
- Proper STR permits/licenses where required

## Typical STR Loan Terms

- **LTV:** Up to 75-80%
- **Min DSCR:** 1.0x using STR income (some accept 0.75x)
- **Credit Score:** 680+ (700+ for best terms)
- **Loan Amounts:** $100K to $3M
- **Terms:** 30-year fixed, 5/6 ARM

## Key Considerations

- **Seasonality:** Lenders want to see full-year data to account for slow seasons
- **Regulations:** Verify the property is in an STR-friendly jurisdiction
- **Management:** Have a management plan (self-manage or property manager)
- **Insurance:** STR-specific insurance is required (standard homeowner's won't cover it)
