## What Is a DSCR Loan?

A **Debt Service Coverage Ratio (DSCR) loan** is a type of mortgage designed specifically for real estate investors. Unlike conventional loans that require W-2s, tax returns, and income verification, DSCR loans qualify you based on one thing: **can the property's rental income cover the mortgage payment?**

That's it. No personal income docs. No DTI calculations. No explaining your last three years of Schedule E.

## How DSCR Is Calculated

The formula is simple:

**DSCR = Monthly Rent / Monthly PITI**

Where PITI includes:
- **P**rincipal payment
- **I**nterest payment
- Property **T**axes (monthly)
- **I**nsurance (monthly)
- HOA dues (if applicable)

### Example

- Purchase Price: $400,000
- Down Payment: 25% ($100,000)
- Loan Amount: $300,000
- Interest Rate: 7.5%
- Monthly Rent: $3,200

Monthly PITI breakdown:
- P&I: $2,098
- Taxes: $350/mo
- Insurance: $150/mo
- **Total PITI: $2,598**

**DSCR = $3,200 / $2,598 = 1.23x**

A 1.23x DSCR means the property generates 23% more income than the mortgage costs. Most lenders want 1.0x or higher, with better rates at 1.25x+.

## What DSCR Ratio Do You Need?

- **1.25x+** — Strong. Best rates and terms available.
- **1.0x - 1.24x** — Workable. Most lenders will fund this, but expect slightly higher rates.
- **0.75x - 0.99x** — Some lenders offer "no-ratio" or sub-1.0 DSCR programs. Higher rates, larger down payment.
- **Below 0.75x** — Difficult. Consider restructuring the deal.

## Who Uses DSCR Loans?

- Self-employed investors whose tax returns don't reflect true income
- W-2 employees adding rental properties to their portfolio
- Foreign nationals investing in US real estate
- LLCs and entities (most DSCR loans close in entity name)
- Investors with 5+ financed properties (conventional caps at 10)

## Typical DSCR Loan Terms

- **LTV:** Up to 80% (20-25% down typical)
- **Credit Score:** 660-700 minimum (varies by lender)
- **Loan Amounts:** $75K to $3M+ (some go to $5M)
- **Property Types:** SFR, condo, townhome, 2-4 unit, small multifamily
- **Terms:** 30-year fixed, 5/6 ARM, interest-only options
- **Prepayment:** Varies — some have 3-5 year PPP, others have none

## How to Get the Best DSCR Rate

1. **Maximize your DSCR ratio** — higher rent or lower purchase price improves your ratio
2. **Put more down** — 25-30% down gets better pricing than 20%
3. **Credit score matters** — 740+ unlocks the best tiers
4. **Choose your prepayment** — accepting a longer PPP often means a lower rate
